Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [November 23, 2025]

VishnyxLabs ("we", "our", "us") builds SaaS platforms, AI solutions, and provides software consulting and training. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out contact or inquiry forms
  • Request a free audit, demo, or consultation
  • Engage in consulting or SaaS services
  • Join training programs or workshops

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address & approximate location
  • Device type, browser type, OS
  • Pages visited & time spent

1.3 Cookies

We use cookies for analytics, performance, and session preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work correctly.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries & communicate with you

  • Ensure security, detect misuse, and maintain performance
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Legal Basis

  • Performance of a contract

  • Legal obligations

4. Sharing Information

We may share data with:

We retain data only as long as needed for business, contractual, or legal purposes. When no longer required, data is deleted or anonymized.
